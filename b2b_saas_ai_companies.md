# B2B SaaS AI Companies by Vertical (Beginner Guide)

## Audit & Compliance
- **Vanta** — Automates security compliance tasks and evidence collection for audits.
- **Drata** — Helps companies monitor controls and stay audit-ready continuously.
- **Secureframe** — Guides teams through compliance and keeps policies up to date.

## Legal
- **Ironclad** — Uses AI to create, review, and manage contracts faster.
- **Lexion** — Organizes and searches contracts so teams can find key terms quickly.
- **Spellbook** — Suggests contract language and edits inside Microsoft Word.

## Finance & Accounting
- **Ramp** — Uses AI to detect spend issues and automate expense reporting.
- **Brex** — Automates card spend controls and finance workflows for companies.
- **Vic.ai** — Automates invoice processing and approval for accounts payable.

## HR & Recruiting
- **Paradox** — AI chat assistant that schedules interviews and answers candidate questions.
- **Eightfold** — Matches people to roles using skills data and career paths.
- **SeekOut** — Helps recruiters find and engage candidates with AI search.

## Sales
- **Gong** — Analyzes sales calls to coach teams and improve deals.
- **Clari** — Forecasts revenue and highlights deal risks using AI.
- **Outreach** — Automates sales emails and sequences to reach prospects.

## Customer Support
- **Zendesk AI** — Suggests replies and routes tickets to the right agents.
- **Intercom** — Uses AI chatbots to answer customer questions and hand off to humans.
- **Ada** — Lets companies build AI agents to resolve support requests.

## Marketing
- **Jasper** — Generates marketing copy and campaigns for teams.
- **Writer** — Provides AI writing with brand rules and approval workflows.
- **Mutiny** — Personalizes website content for different B2B audiences.

## Operations & Supply Chain
- **Samsara** — Uses AI to monitor fleets and improve safety and efficiency.
- **Project44** — Tracks shipments and predicts delays in logistics.
- **C3 AI** — Offers AI tools for predictive maintenance and operations.

## IT & Security
- **CrowdStrike** — Uses AI to detect and stop cybersecurity threats.
- **Darktrace** — Learns normal network behavior and flags anomalies.
- **Okta** — Automates identity and access management for companies.

## Data & Analytics
- **Databricks** — Provides an AI data platform for building models and analytics.
- **Snowflake** — Cloud data platform with AI features for insights and apps.
- **Sigma** — Lets teams analyze data with spreadsheets and AI assistance.

## Product & Engineering
- **GitHub Copilot** — Suggests code and helps developers write faster.
- **Snyk** — Finds vulnerabilities and fixes issues in code and dependencies.
- **LinearB** — Uses AI to improve engineering workflows and delivery.
